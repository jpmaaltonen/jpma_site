About

29. February 2020 — By jpma

Hello!

My name is Matias. I’ve always been interested in tech, tinkering and electronics. This is a place where I ramble about my ongoing projects and tech related stuff that interest me.

I’ve never been keen on writing anything, I’m more of a talker. Even writing e-mails feels unnatural to me. I think e-mails should be coherent and clear and with my lacking writing skill it takes way too long to write them. Lacking skills in written expression I also find making documentation hard. My notes are usually horrible, incoherent mess, which works fine as long as they are just my own notes and I don’t have decipher them after a long time has passed.

So to practice writing and documenting stuff I made this site. The posts here will most likely be notes and reminders mainly for myself. But it would be cool if on the off chance someone else finds them helpful or interesting.

You can check out my Github-page for any related material. In example the source for this site is there.

If you need to contact me for some reason, you can try and email me at: jpma at jpma dot fi
